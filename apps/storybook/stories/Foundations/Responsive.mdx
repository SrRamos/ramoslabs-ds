<div className="responsive-section">
  <h2 className="responsive-section-title">Design Principles</h2>

  <div className="principle-card">
    <div className="principle-card-title">
      📱 Mobile First - Because Fans Discover on the Go
    </div>
    <div className="principle-card-content">
      Most fans first see an event on their phone, waiting in line, scrolling before bed, or checking a friend's share.
      Start with mobile, then enhance. The excitement should hit just as hard on a 5-inch screen.
    </div>
  </div>

  <div className="principle-card">
    <div className="principle-card-title">
      🎯 Intrinsic Sizing - Let Content Breathe
    </div>
    <div className="principle-card-content">
      Use <code>min()</code>, <code>max()</code>, <code>clamp()</code>, and <code>auto-fit</code> before reaching for media queries.
      Event cards, artist names, venue details, let them find their natural rhythm. Less breakpoints, more flow.
    </div>
  </div>

  <div className="principle-card">
    <div className="principle-card-title">
      📐 Major Shifts Only - Don't Interrupt the Journey
    </div>
    <div className="principle-card-content">
      Only use breakpoints for major layout changes (sidebar → stacked, grid → single column).
      A fan scrolling through events shouldn't notice the design working, they should just feel it working.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
